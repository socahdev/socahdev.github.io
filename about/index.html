About

For every historical age, it's primary means of technological reproduction is mirrored in the leading artform of the time. Thus the heyday of the literary novel coincided with the nineteenth century, when industrial printing techniques were at their most accessible — only to give way to the age of motion picture as soon as the grand scale duplication and distribution of film stock became feasible. Today, as we have entered the information age, it is only conceivable that the medium of the video game is set to take over as the predominant popular artform.

The S.O.C.A.H. is a loosely defined workgroup dedicated to making indie video games. It was founded in 2015 in the forest clad village of Hilversum, and will therefore always be slightly dedicated to modernist aesthetics.
