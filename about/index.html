About

The S.O.C.A.H. is a loosely defined workgroup dedicated to making video games and music. It was founded in Hilversum in 2015, and it’s members are primarily focused along the Hilversum - Utrecht - Nieuwegein axis in the Netherlands. It also has members in New York, Berlin and Seoul.
