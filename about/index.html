About

And it was in the end of times, as we wasted away, reading Bergotte and drinking old wine. We had already paid our last regards to the decaying world.

The S.O.C.A.H. is an independently operating, loosely defined, international workgroup, dedicated to making arthouse video games. It was founded in the year 2015 in the forest clad hamlet of Hilversum, and will therefore always be slightly dedicated to modernist aesthetics.

At S.O.C.A.H. we confidently argue that for every historical age, it's primary means of technological reproduction is reflected in the leading popular artform of the time. The nineteenth century, for instance, when industrial printing techniques became widely accessible, was the heyday of the literary novel. Later, after the dawn of the age of film, the leading artform shifted towards movies and television drama. Now, in the information age, we believe that in a similar vein, the medium of the video game is set to become the primary means of storytelling of the time.

Through our games, the S.O.C.A.H. attempts to contribute to this development. Get in contact with us if you'd like to collaborate.
