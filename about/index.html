About

>> our latest game the raintime is out now! get it for free at steam or itch.io

And it was in the end of times, as they wasted away and drank old wine. They had already paid their last regards to the decaying world.

The S.O.C.A.H. is an independently operating, loosely defined workgroup, dedicated to making arthouse video games. It was founded in 2015 in the forest clad village of Hilversum, and will therefore always be slightly dedicated to modernist aesthetics.

As our founding principle we confidently argue that for every historical age it's primary means of technological reproduction is reflected in the leading artform of the time. The nineteenth century, for instance, when industrial printing techniques became widely accessible, was the heyday of the literary novel. Later, after the dawn of the age of film, the leading popular artform shifted towards film and television drama. Now, in the information age, we believe that in a similar vein the medium of the video game is set to take over as the primary artform of the time.

Please get in contact if you'd like to work with us.
