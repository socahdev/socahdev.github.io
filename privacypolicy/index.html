  • Deluge: Threnody of Waves privacy policy
1. Introduction

Welcome to Deluge: Threnody of Waves, a visual novel game designed for entertainment purposes. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to that information.

2. Information Collection and Use

To the best of our knowledge, our app does not collect any personal data or prompt user input. We do not use third-party services or SDKs for analytics, ads, or in-app purchases. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we ensure that no personal information is gathered or stored by our app.

3. Permissions

Our app requires basic permissions to function correctly. We do not access or use your device's camera, microphone, or any other sensitive permissions.

4. Age Restrictions

Deluge: Threnody of Waves is designed for users aged 7 and above, with a target audience of 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

5. Crash Logs and Error Reports

While we do not collect crash logs or error reports personally, please note that Google Play may collect such data to help improve the performance and reliability of apps available on its platform. This data collection is subject to Google's privacy policy.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page. You are advised to review this privacy policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@socah.org
